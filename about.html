About


The passion for wings started once with the one for flying in 2012 when I had a casual tandem flight. That was the moment when I knew I have to start flying. A couple of months later I started the flying school. Was my first step in search of my dream. In my first five days of school I've learned how to control the wing on the ground, couldn't wait to feel it in the air, every moment I was wondering when I'll be prepaired

for my first solo flight. Surprisely at the end of the fifth day the trainer took me on a hill, I knew the moment I was expecting for so long, is there. After few minutes of briefing and a few failed take off's I managed to fly.. I still remember that feeling like was few moments ago and I will remember it for the rest of my life. That is how a dream can became reality.If you wanna live your dream you can start from here.
